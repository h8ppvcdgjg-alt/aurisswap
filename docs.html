How it Works

AurisSwap is a decentralized exchange designed to ensure long-term sustainability by promoting real liquidity, governance, and rewards for stakeholders.

How to Swap Tokens

To swap tokens on AurisSwap:

  • Select the token pair (e.g., AURIS/BNB)
  • Input the amount you want to swap
  • Review the fees and expected slippage
  • Confirm the swap
  • Your tokens will be exchanged instantly on the blockchain

How to Provide Liquidity

By providing liquidity to pools on AurisSwap, you help ensure the smooth operation of token swaps and earn a share of the fees. To provide liquidity:

  • Choose a pool you want to contribute to
  • Deposit equal value of both tokens into the pool
  • Earn rewards proportional to your share of the pool

How to Stake AURIS

Staking AURIS allows you to earn rewards while supporting the ecosystem. To stake:

  • Connect your wallet to the staking page
  • Select how many AURIS tokens you want to stake
  • Confirm and start earning staking rewards

How to Farm on AurisSwap

Farming allows you to earn more tokens by staking liquidity pool tokens. To farm:

  • Deposit liquidity into a farm pool
  • Earn rewards from farming the liquidity
  • Withdraw farming rewards and reinvest or claim them
