Treasury

The AurisSwap Treasury is the financial backbone of the protocol. It exists to ensure long-term sustainability, security and continuous development.

Purpose of the Treasury

Funds accumulated in the Treasury are used to support the ongoing operation and evolution of AurisSwap.

  • Protocol development and maintenance
  • Infrastructure, hosting and operational costs
  • Security audits and risk management
  • Liquidity and ecosystem incentives
  • Marketing, partnerships and growth initiatives
  • Core contributor and founding team compensation
  • Strategic reserves

Treasury Management

During the early stages of AurisSwap, the Treasury is managed by the core team to allow rapid development, fast decision-making and efficient execution.

As the protocol matures, treasury management may evolve toward:

  • Multi-signature control
  • Increased transparency reporting
  • Governance-based allocation mechanisms

Transparency & Evolution

AurisSwap is committed to transparency and long-term alignment. Treasury usage policies may evolve over time to reflect governance, security requirements and ecosystem growth.

Detailed allocation reports may be published as the protocol scales.
