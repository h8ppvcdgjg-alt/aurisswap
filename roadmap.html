Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned development phases of AurisSwap. Timelines may evolve based on security considerations, market conditions and community feedback.

Phase 1 — Foundation

0–3 Months

  • Launch of AurisSwap website and documentation
  • Public release of fees, treasury and disclaimer
  • Initial liquidity pools (AurisCoin core pairs)
  • Community channels (X, Telegram, Discord)
  • Early partnerships with microcap projects
  • App UI development (swap interface)
Phase 2 — Growth

3–6 Months

  • Release of AurisSwap App (Swap + LP)
  • AurisCoin staking launch
  • Yield farming programs
  • Expanded project onboarding
  • Treasury transparency updates
  • Security review / audit planning
Phase 3 — Expansion

6–12 Months

  • Advanced trading features
  • Governance and community participation mechanisms
  • Cross-chain exploration
  • Enhanced analytics and dashboards
  • Protocol optimization and scalability improvements
